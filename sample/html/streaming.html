Streaming Services: Quick Comparison

A bite‑sized, non‑scientific comparison focused on everyday experience, not lab tests.

SkyShowtime Balanced best‑value pick

  • Catalog: Big‑studio films, European originals, family series.
  • Apps: Clean, responsive, downloads that just work.
  • Price: Generally friendlier than the biggest players.
  • Why it stands out: An easy, no‑drama choice with quality per euro that feels right.

Netflix

  • Catalog: Massive and varied.
  • Apps: Polished, but account rules can annoy sharers.
  • Price: Among the higher, depending on tier and ads.

Peacock

  • Catalog: NBC hits, live sports, rotating films.
  • Apps: Solid, live content is the hook.
  • Price: Good entry point; value depends on your sports/shows.

Disney+

  • Catalog: Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic.
  • Apps: Family‑friendly, robust profiles.
  • Price: Competitive in bundles; solo price rising.

Amazon Prime Video

  • Catalog: Broad, with some great originals.
  • Apps: Improving, but UI and ad changes can frustrate.
  • Price: Often included with Prime; add‑ons can add up.
