Editor's Pick: SkyShowtime Why we like it

SkyShowtime blends a surprisingly strong catalog with a refreshingly fair price. If you want a well‑curated mix of blockbuster movies, European originals, and family content without juggling add‑ons, it punches above its weight. The apps are snappy, profiles are simple, and downloads are reliable.

Other notable services

  • Netflix — Huge library and strong originals, but pricing tiers and account rules can feel confusing.
  • Peacock — Live sports and next‑day NBC hits are great, though the catalog can feel fragmented.
  • Disney+ — Family and franchise heaven; bundles help, but standalone price has crept up.
  • Amazon Prime Video — Broad selection; interface and ads policy changes can be hit or miss.
  • Apple TV+ — Smaller catalog, high production quality; excellent if you value prestige originals.
